what is split the Bill ?

split the Bill is a program that allows it's users to share thier bill online with thier freinds and see the exact amount they owe for thier items without the hassle of calculating it themselves the process is easy just take a picture of your bill share the room number with your friends so they can join and then each user can pick the items they bought and the app creates a personalized bill for each user

who are we ?

we are students from telhai university and this is our final project our goal with this project is to help users keep track of their money by making it easier for each person to figure out how much they owe a place of business and the way we are doing that is by allowing users to share and Itemize thier Bill
